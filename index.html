SEASONAL FRUITS & BENEFITS

  1. Summer

    1. Mango - It may improve heart health
    2. Musk Melon - Improves eye health
    3. Blackberries - Boosts brain health
    4. plum - Reduces the chances of high blood pressure

  2. Spring

    1. cherries - Helps in relaxing the mind and improves sleep quality
    2. Jackfruit - Strengthens bones
    3. Pineapple - It may reduce the risk of cancer
    4. Strawberries - It may help in lowering blood pressure
    5. Lychee - Enhances the production of red blood cells

  3. Winter

    1. Custard Apple- May help in boosting the mood
    2. Apple - Maintains healthy blood sugar levels.
    3. Jamun - Improves hemoglobin
    4. Peach - Reduces inflammation
    5. Pear - May help in improving blood circulation

  4. Rainy

    1. Dates - Contains diseases fighting antioxidants
    2. kiwi - Helps in the treatment of asthma
    3. Orange - Maintains blood sugar level.
    4. Grapes - Beneficial for people with chronic diseases
    5. Fig - May aid digestion and improve gut health
